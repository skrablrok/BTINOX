SERVICES

Process system fabrication +

We are experts in the fabrication of SKID systems, high-pressure vessels, platforms and walkways, as well as associated stainless steel (inox) equipment for the pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, chemical, and food industries

Production and assembly +

Fabrication and installation of stainless steel structures, process equipment, and piping in compliance with the highest hygiene and safety standards.

TIG welding +

Precision TIG welding of stainless steel for high-pressure and sanitary systems, suitable for the pharmaceutical and food industries.

Orbital welding +

Automated orbital welding for perfect, clean, and repeatable pipe joints without contamination.

Polishing and grinding +

Mechanical and electropolishing of internal and external surfaces to achieve optimal roughness and easy cleaning.

Pipeline welding +

Installation and welding of sanitary piping systems with guaranteed tightness and long-lasting corrosion resistance.

Maintenance +

Regular and emergency maintenance of process equipment and piping to ensure continuous and safe production operation.

ABOUT THE COMPANY

Our company is independent and built on many years of experience gained both in domestic and international markets. We specialize in welding services, assembly work, and various mechanical and processing operations.

Our expertise and dedication place us among reliable partners, especially in demanding industrial sectors such as the pharmaceutical, food, and chemical industries. Over the years, we have developed a unique approach where welding is not just a technical procedure but a true art of joining materials into a solid, functional, and durable whole.

We approach each project individually, with a comprehensive understanding of our clients’ needs. Our services are based on advanced technologies and innovative processes that ensure quality, reliability, and long-lasting solutions.

We recognize that the modern industrial environment demands high flexibility, rapid responsiveness, and above all, precise execution. Therefore, we prioritize professionalism, responsibility, and continuous investment in employee knowledge and training.

Why choose our company?

High Quality: All our services are performed according to the highest quality standards. Every execution is the result of meticulous planning and careful implementation.

Innovation: We continuously monitor technological developments and implement modern solutions that provide our clients with a competitive advantage.

Responsibility and Precision: We approach each project with responsibility, understanding the importance of safety, durability, and flawless execution.

Flexibility: Our teams quickly adapt to the requirements and specific conditions on-site. We operate within various industrial environments without interrupting existing processes.

Reliable Business Partner: The trust of our clients is highly valued. We build long-term collaborations based on transparency, respect, and mutual understanding.
