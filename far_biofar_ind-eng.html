PROCESS VESSELS

WIP-CIP-SIP UNITS

A key part of high-quality production is an integrated WIP/CIP/SIP system. Btinox ensures efficient and timely cleaning and sterilization procedures, compliant with customer requirements. We offer a wide range of solutions – from mobile and standalone cleaning systems to integrated CIP satellite systems.

▼

CIP and SIP systems are designed for automatic basic cleaning and disinfection, without unnecessary time lost for assembly and disassembly.

Process optimization depends on effective and reliable cleaning. CIP systems can be integrated into the entire pharmaceutical equipment, including solid and liquid dosing systems. Automation of cleaning processes ensures repeatability, allows validation, and reduces downtime.

The most effective way to implement CIP technology is through its planned integration into the process. When incorporating spray systems, tank cleaners, nozzles, and seals for cleaning automation, we consider all parts of your system – from input to emptying.

Automation of the cleaning cycle primarily transforms batch pharmaceutical processes into continuous operation of production and cleaning cycles.

The advantages of a CIP system include:

  • shorter cleaning cycles
  • optimized use of detergent and water
  • elimination of manual cleaning

Our frameworks for CIP/WIP systems are designed, engineered, automated, and tested. This comprehensive approach ensures open communication between experts and rapid response to any deviations during the project. Each project is managed by a project/process engineer who professionally oversees discussions regarding specific requirements, system integration, and final FAT protocols.

HEATING AND COOLING SYSTEMS

Temperature control and product mixing during the process are key to achieving successful and repeatable results.

Btinox ensures that the product temperature in the vessel remains within a narrow range (+/- 0.5 °C), preventing undesired changes in the product during production. We achieve a high level of temperature control and product homogeneity through the use of state-of-the-art heat transfer technology and careful mixing. Precise measuring devices monitor all key parameters, such as pH, O₂, temperature, and mixing speed throughout the entire process.
